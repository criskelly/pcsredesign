Student Benefits

At Portland Code School, we want to help students reach their goals, regardless of their current level of knowledge or outside responsibilities. As a result, Portland Code School students gain access to many tools, services, and a customized learning environment to support them in their journey to level up their programming skills.

In addition to college-level professional instruction, student benefits include:

  • Childcare provided by WeVillage, on your schedule
  • Treehouse accounts
  • One-on-one instruction
  • Introduction to the Portland tech community
  • Tours of tech companies in Portland
  • Portfolio development
  • Mock interviews, resume review, and job search coaching
  • Catered Saturday sessions
  • Team building sessions
  • Be surrounded by peers learning the same material
  • Supportive learning environment
